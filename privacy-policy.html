Privacy Policy

ChitraBook AI is built for photographers to manage studio workflow, client delivery, gallery access and AI-assisted face search.

We collect only the information required to run the service, such as account details, studio profile, client/event workflow data, uploaded gallery media, payment records and access/session information needed for secure gallery delivery.

Gallery access can be controlled through links, QR codes, client tokens, device limits and photographer sharing controls. Sensitive production security policies may be updated as the product moves toward public launch.

For privacy questions, contact the ChitraBook AI team through the Contact Us page.
