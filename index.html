FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ChitraBook AI?

ChitraBook AI is a complete studio workflow platform for photographers. It helps manage quotations, proposals, bookings, payments, photo uploads, gallery delivery, AI face scan, website builder and earnings.

Who can use ChitraBook AI?

It is built for wedding photographers, event photographers, studios, freelancers and photography teams.

How does AI Face Scan work?

Guests scan their face and ChitraBook AI shows only the photos where their face matches. Clients and photographers can also use face scan to filter gallery photos.

Can clients access the full gallery?

Yes. Clients can access the full event gallery using a secure token, public link or QR code.

Can guests see the full gallery?

No. Guest access is designed for face scan based matched-photo access.

Can I stop gallery sharing?

Yes. The photographer can stop or resume gallery sharing anytime.

Can I regenerate a client token?

Yes. Regenerating a token replaces the old token and resets the 2-device access limit.

Is there an Android app?

Yes. ChitraBook AI is mobile-first and Android-ready through the app workflow.

Can I create quotations and proposals?

Yes. You can create quotations, convert them into proposals, and continue the workflow toward booking and payments.

Can I track payments?

Yes. You can track advance, balance, pending payments, monthly revenue and invoices.

Can I sell photos?

Yes. Guest photo purchases and photographer earnings flow are part of the platform.

Is my gallery private?

Gallery access is controlled through link, QR, token, role and face scan based access.
